Synopsis

Three orphaned boys grow up together, forming an unbreakable brotherhood. One day, they meet a kind-hearted girl who soon becomes like a sister to them. As her marriage approaches, the boys struggle to earn money in a short term and are drawn into the dangerous gang. When a powerful gang leader falsely accuses their sister, the brothers are forced into a violent confrontation. In a tragic turn of events, the gang leader is killed—now his influential father seeks revenge, setting the stage for a gripping battle of loyalty, justice, and survival. On the run and hideouts. System vs survival. Questions raised: Will police catch them? Will gang kill them? Will they surrender? Are they criminals… or victims? “Every move in life is a gamble… But some gambles cost everything.” 👉 PAGADE
